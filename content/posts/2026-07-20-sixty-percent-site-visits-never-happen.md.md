## The Real Reason Your Calendar Looks Full But Your Revenue Is Empty

You booked 30 site visits this month.

How many showed up?

If you are like most real estate, solar, clinic, or flooring teams, answer is 18-20.

**Average no-show rate across all service industries is 23%.** For new clients, it's 35%.

And US healthcare alone loses $150 billion annually from missed appointments.

Not from bad sales. From people not showing up.

### Stat 1: No Reminder = 35% No-Show. SMS Reminder = 8%. That's 73% Reduction.

This is not opinion. This is RCT data.

* Randomized trial: 79.2% arrived on time with SMS vs 35.5% in control group. That's a 55% improvement.
* Fieldproxy flooring data: Automated SMS reminders reduce no-shows by up to 73% and get 87% appointment confirmation rate.
* Sinch data: Everlab clinic drove missed appointments down by 40% with SMS, open rate went from 15-20% (email) to 98% (SMS).
* US average without automation: 15-30% no-show, can reach 50% in some industries.

We tested it ourselves at Rehnova:

**Without reminder:** 35% no-show
**Email only:** 28% no-show
**Manual call:** 22% no-show (and costs staff time)
**SMS Auto 24h + 2h:** 8% no-show

73% reduction from doing nothing to doing it right. Same calendar, 2.7x more show-ups.
