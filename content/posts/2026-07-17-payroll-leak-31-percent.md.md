## Your Business Has a 31% Leak and It’s Not Showing in QuickBooks

We tracked a 10-person agency for a week. 400 hours total.

Not tasks. Not feelings. Hours, with a timer.

Here is where it went:

![Where time goes - Pie chart](https://res.cloudinary.com/dhcayqpqr/image/upload/v1784269996/20260717_113221_0000_jl5fjp.png)

**Read that again:**
* 31% = 124 hours/week on repeatable copy-paste: lead entry, reminders, reports.
* 29% = 116 hours/week wasted searching for files, asking for updates, status meetings.
* Only 22% = Actual creative, revenue-generating work.

<span class="text-[#1DA9CE] font-semibold">You are paying 31% of your payroll to do work a $20 tool does at 2 AM without coffee.</span>

If your average payroll is $4,000/month, that’s $1,240/month burned. $14,880/year. Invisible.

## The Fix: The 3-Bucket Rule

Stop asking “Should I hire or automate?”

Use buckets.

**Bucket 1: Repeatable (31% - Automate First)**
Same steps every time. If you can write it on a sticky note, automate it.
Examples: Lead FB -> Sheet, Invoice reminders, Appointment SMS, Weekly reports, Backup.

Tool: Zapier, Make, n8n. Cost: $20-75/mo.

**Bucket 2: Wasted (29% - Systemize)**
Searching files, asking “where is this?”, status meetings.
Fix: Single source of truth. Notion / ClickUp / Drive with rules. Auto dashboards.

**Bucket 3: Creative (22% - Protect and Hire For)**
This is where humans win. Sales calls, content, strategy, relationships.
Hire VAs / Team ONLY for this bucket.

<span class="text-[#1DA9CE] font-semibold">Rule: Automate Bucket 1, Systemize Bucket 2, Humanize Bucket 3.</span>

Most businesses do opposite. They hire humans for Bucket 1, ignore Bucket 2, and have no time left for Bucket 3.

## Your 15-Minute Action Today

1. **Audit (5 mins):** List 10 tasks you did yesterday. Tag: R (Repeatable), W (Wasted), C (Creative).
2. **Calculate (5 mins):** Count R tasks x hours/week x hourly rate. That’s your monthly leak.
3. **Replace 1 (5 mins):** Pick the biggest R task. Google “automate [task] with Make.com”. Set it up.

Don’t try to fix all 31%. Fix 5% this week. That’s 20 hours back.
